自 2024 年成立以来，Bili-Board 始终致力于构建公正、公开且深度契合本土 B 站生态数据的 VOCALOID 音乐榜单体系。随着 2025 年业务版图的拓展，Bili-Board_Atel 陆续推出了「传说曲周榜」、「竖屏周榜速递」等细分榜单矩阵，这对品牌视觉系统的兼容性与延展性提出了更高标准。

本次 Bili-Board 2025 全系视觉包装项目，旨在解决多频道并发下的视觉统一性问题。在设计策略上，我们审慎延续了上一代「简约、柔和、直观」的品牌视觉基因，以确保核心用户群体对品牌认知的连续性与稳定性。

设计的核心突破点在于信息可视化系统的重构。针对新加入的竖屏媒介与多元化数据维度，我们对版面逻辑进行了深度优化。通过重塑数据排版规范，我们在保持画面呼吸感的同时，大幅提升了高密度信息在不同终端的易读性与传达效率，成功实现了从单一榜单向综合性数据厂牌的视觉进化。



Since its founding in 2024, Bili-Board has been dedicated to building a fair, transparent, and deeply integrated VOCALOID music ranking system tailored to the Bilibili ecosystem. With the expansion of its 2025 business scope, Bili-Board_Atel has successively launched segmented ranking matrices such as the "Legend Songs Weekly" and "Vertical Video Weekly Express," which pose new demands on the brand's visual system in terms of compatibility and extensibility.

This Bili-Board 2025 comprehensive visual packaging project aims to address the issue of visual consistency across multiple concurrent channels. In terms of design strategy, we have prudently continued the previous generation's brand visual DNA of "simplicity, softness, and intuitiveness" to ensure the continuity and stability of brand recognition among core user groups.

The core breakthrough in the design lies in the restructuring of the information visualization system. In response to the newly added vertical media and diversified data dimensions, we have deeply optimized the layout logic. By reshaping data typesetting standards, we have significantly improved the readability and transmission efficiency of high-density information across different terminals while maintaining visual breathing space, successfully achieving a visual evolution from a single ranking to a comprehensive data brand.
