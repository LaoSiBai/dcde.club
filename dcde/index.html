DCDE (Design Crew for Design Enthusiasts) 是一个致力于探索中学生设计美学的实验性组织。

在这个项目中，我们试图建立一种全新的视觉语言，将严谨的网格系统与流动的创意表达相结合。 Logo 的设计源自于标准化的几何图形，象征着秩序与规则；而动态的排版和色彩运用，则代表了在规则之下的无限变量与可能性。

核心视觉识别系统不仅应用于数字化平台，还将延展至实体周边、展览物料等多种媒介，构建一个完整、立体的品牌形象。



DCDE (Design Crew for Design Enthusiasts) is an experimental organization dedicated to exploring design aesthetics among high school students.

In this project, we attempt to establish a new visual language that combines a rigorous grid system with fluid creative expression. The logo design is derived from standardized geometric shapes, symbolizing order and rules; while dynamic typography and color application represent infinite variables and possibilities under those rules.

The core visual identity system is applied not only to digital platforms but also extends to physical merchandise, exhibition materials, and various other media, building a complete and three-dimensional brand image.
